# Customer Churn Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview
The Customer Churn Intelligence Dashboard is an advanced Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze customer churn patterns, identify high-risk customer segments, and generate actionable business insights for customer retention strategies.

This project leverages interactive visual analytics, AI-driven insights, and executive-level reporting techniques to support data-driven decision-making in the banking/customer relationship domain.

---

# Business Objective
The primary objective of this project is to:

- Identify key factors influencing customer churn
- Analyze churn behavior across demographics and regions
- Detect high-risk customer groups
- Provide strategic business recommendations for retention improvement
- Enable executives and analysts to make informed business decisions through interactive dashboards

# Business Insights

- Germany region demonstrated the highest customer churn concentration
- Inactive customers showed significantly higher churn probability
- Customers with fewer banking products exhibited increased attrition risk
- Mid-age customer groups required targeted engagement strategies
- High-balance customers represented critical retention priority segments

---

# Strategic Recommendations

- Implement targeted retention campaigns for inactive customers
- Increase customer engagement initiatives in high-risk regions
- Promote multi-product adoption strategies to improve retention
- Develop personalized retention programs for high-value customers
- Strengthen customer relationship initiatives for mid-age demographics
